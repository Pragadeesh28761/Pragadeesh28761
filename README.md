## About Me

I am a final-year **B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering** student based in **Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India**, working toward a career as a **Software Engineer**. My focus is on **backend engineering** — designing clean REST APIs, modeling reliable data layers, and building systems that hold up under real-world load.

I work primarily with **Java and Spring Boot** on the backend, backed by **MySQL** for persistence, and I extend that with **Python** and **JavaScript** for scripting, automation, and full-stack delivery. I am actively building cloud fluency with **AWS**, and I approach every project with an engineering mindset: readable code, sound architecture, and systems designed to scale rather than just to work.

Alongside project work, I am consistently sharpening my **Data Structures & Algorithms** foundation through structured practice on LeetCode, HackerRank, and CodeChef — treating problem-solving as a core engineering discipline, not a separate track.

I am currently **open to Internship and Full-Time Software Engineer roles**, particularly in backend-heavy, cloud-native environments where I can contribute to production systems and grow alongside experienced engineering teams.
